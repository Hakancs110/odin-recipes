Donut

Description

A stylized donut model that doesn't look very realistic

Ingredients

  • Computer
  • Modeling Program
  • Internet
  • a pinch of tutorials

Steps

  1. Step 1: put some texture, some cylinder, some color and sugar in a cabinet.
  2. Step 2: Stir until it turns brown in color.
  3. Step 3: put some internet until the color clears
  4. Last Step: some sugar on it and the donut is ready
